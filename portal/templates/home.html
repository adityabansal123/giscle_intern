Lane Keeping

GISCLE uses onboard solutions which can sense the vehicle deviating from its lane, the system helps the car stay on course near the center of the lane by continuously applying a small amount of counter-steering force

Sleep and Distraction Warning

Driver Alert Realsense uses the unique pupil,eye,mouth and other facial identification technology to detect and analyze the drowsiness of driver. When it judges a driver in fatigue status, it will provide warning alarm to remind the driver.
